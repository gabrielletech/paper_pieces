How it all started

Herboratum greenery was founded in 2013 by Joel Kasper & Ana Bayaku. Joel grew up in the greenery business as his parents owned one of the most succesful greeneries in Vermont. Ana fell in love with the job (and Joel) after they met in 2011.
They work together to share their belief that plants in your home and work spaces will increase your quality of life -- you can’t disagree with that!
